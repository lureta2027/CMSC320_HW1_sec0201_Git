
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git helps with collaboration and creating different versions of your project.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: It could be applied to healthcare. For example, a hospital could use it for analyzing patient data.


Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines branches and keeps the history of both branches. Rebasing rewrite the commit history by applying changes from one branch onto another.
